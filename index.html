Why Ridegain

Your phone stays portrait, numbers-first and ultra-light — so the only thing between you and the effort is the effort.

Numbers, front and center

Power, heart rate and cadence in big digits. See your target, the gap to it, and whether you're in-zone at a glance.

Workouts & ERG mode

Presets — Sweet Spot, VO₂ Max, Endurance, FTP Test — plus a simple editor for your own. ERG mode sets the resistance for you.

Fitness tracking

CTL / ATL / TSB for form and fatigue, plus W/kg, a power profile (5s · 1m · 5m · 20m) and rider-type analysis.

Automatic Strava sync

Indoor rides upload themselves — so your outdoor rides, runs and other apps all live in one place on Strava.
