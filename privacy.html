Ridegain Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Ridegain ("the App") is an indoor cycling training app provided by its developer, Ridegain ("we", "us"). This policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Overview

The App stores your data only on your device as a rule. We do not operate any account system that collects information identifying you (such as your name or email address), and we do not accumulate your personal data on any server we operate.

The App displays no advertising and uses no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs whatsoever.

2. Information We Store

The App stores the following information on your device. It is not sent to our servers.

TypeDetailsLocation
Workout recordsThe content, results, and history of your training sessionsOn-device storage
Sensor readingsExercise data such as heart rate, power, and cadence obtained from Bluetooth-connected sensors or smart trainersOn-device storage
App settingsDisplay language, units, and other preferencesOn-device storage
Strava connectionAuthentication credentials such as access tokens, if you connect StravaEncrypted on-device storage (Secure Store)

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location (GPS)
  • Personally identifying information such as name, email address, or phone number (we have no account registration)
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA / AAID)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking

4. Permissions

  • Bluetooth (BLUETOOTH_SCAN / BLUETOOTH_CONNECT): To discover and connect to sensors such as heart-rate monitors, power meters, and smart trainers, and to obtain exercise data.

5. Strava Integration (Optional)

Only if you choose to enable Strava integration:

  • Your workout records (activities) in the App are uploaded to your Strava account.
  • For authorization (OAuth), token exchange with Strava's authentication servers is performed via a relay server we operate (Cloudflare Workers). This relay server only passes the request through and does not store or log your tokens or any personal data.
  • Data sent to Strava is handled under Strava's Privacy Policy.

You can disconnect Strava at any time within the App. If you do not connect Strava, none of the above data transfer occurs.

6. Deleting Your Data

  • Disconnecting Strava within the App removes the stored credentials.
  • The App's data-deletion feature or uninstalling the App deletes all data stored on your device.
  • To delete activities already uploaded to Strava, please do so yourself on Strava.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age set by your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Regional Laws

  • EU / UK (GDPR): As noted above, the App does not accumulate personal data on our servers and processes data on your device. On-device data remains under your control and can be deleted by you.
  • California, USA (CCPA / CPRA): We do not sell your personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy as needed. If we make significant changes, we will announce them on this page.
